I'm feeling a bit scared to blog, right now. I want this to be perfect. I want every post to be perfect, I want my blog to be perfect. Which is weird, because I am no perfectionist.

I have a few thousand things I could write about, but nothing seems appropriate for this moment.

Did you know I've had many blogs, many years ago? I liked swapping between blog platforms, and buying new domain names… this is a trait which has followed me through life, notably with cameras, and latterly with PKM/notes apps. I still haven't worked out what's going on here.

But surely, the name of the game is “COMMIT”. Commit, Dave. Commit to one thing. One notes app. One camera. One blog. You can do this.

And then, regarding the blogging thing, just write. Write whatever comes to your mind to write. Don't second guess who's going to read or what their opinion is going to be, or anything like that. Just write. Write write write.
